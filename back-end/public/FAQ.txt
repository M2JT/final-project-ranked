Frequently Asked Questions
Q: How do I create a new game thread?
A: You can submit your request to the Ranked administrators by filling out the request form on the
Request a Thread
page in order to create a new game thread for the game that you and your friends are interested in.
Q: How do I create an account?
A: To register an Ranked account, you can click the hamburger menu in the top right corner and click
Register
Q: How do I change my account password?
A: To change your Ranked account password, you can simply click the
Reset Password
button in your
Account
page.
Q: How do I delete my account?
A: To deactivate your Ranked account, you can click the
Deactivate Account
button in your
Account
page.
